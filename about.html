who am i ?

as you must have understood by now, my name is padmanabhan subramanian. i work as a specialist for automotive infotainment tuner at BOSCH. a tricky question i get held at is what i am passionate about? it is hard to be passionate about just one thing, isn’t it?

i enjoy design. who doesn’t, right? anyone gets passionate about design when they see one they like. I enjoy well thought out graphics design, anything minimalistic, creative and unusual architecture design, and a well detailed 3D design. I spend some time sketching or learning blender, gimp, and figma.

I enjoy programming. still, a long way to go before I get my approval of being a good one at it. Getting hands dirty with programming languages like C, C++, Python, and web development languages like HTML, CSS and Javascript give me joy!

I enjoy teaching. I believe everyone should share what they know. It only proves what one doesn’t know. I have thought basics of programming and 3D at a computer institute after my education and it was fulfilling. I still get to teach a few engineering students basics of programming and software versioning over the weekends.

I enjoy riding my motorcycle. Riding my motorcycle gives me time to think and reflect on many things I would never ponder during my daily routine. Uncertainty during a ride is always I look forward to

I enjoy learning. Learning about a lot of things; a lot of unrelated things if you ask. For work, I learn about the SAFe agile process and tuner module. I love cars and the technology which goes into cars. As a passion, I learn more about programming, the design of many things, and a bit of German language which isn’t moving too far.
